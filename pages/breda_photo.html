Now in its second decade, BredaPhoto has grown into the largest photo festival in the Benelux. Among its key highlights is the International Talent Program (ITP), an initiative that brings together emerging artists from ten prestigious European art academies. 


At its core, this project was about connecting audiences to the bold vision of BredaPhoto and the ITP and bringing new creative voices to the forefront.
